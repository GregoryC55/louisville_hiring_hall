About

LouisvilleHiringHall.com is a group of individuals who play the BattleTech boardgame in the Louisville, KY and southern Indiana area. We support all rulesets for the game, but focus on Total Warfare and Alpha Strike rules. We also support all eras of play so everyone is welcome!
