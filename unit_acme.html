1st Acme Rangers

Unit History

The 1st Acme Rangers were formed from the remnants of multiple mercenary and planetary milita commands that had been destroyed during the Jihad. The were brought together by Colonel John Smith, a former milita commander from the world of Tybalt. John Smith was not happy with the decision by the Federated Suns to surrender his world, among others, to the newborn Republic of the Sphere. He and his unit have just returned from a year long garrison contract with the Lyran Commonwealth and are currently looking for further contracts.
